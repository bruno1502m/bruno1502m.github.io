ABOUT

We are dedicated to bringing together coffeelovers on campus!

Our goal is to create a welcoming community where students can share their love of coffee and learn from each other.

Throughout the year, CoffeeHub hosts a variety of events and activities that cater to all levels of coffee enthusiasts. From coffee tastings and brewing workshops to trips to local coffee shops and roasteries, there is always something new to explore and learn. We also welcome guest speakers who can share their expertise and insights on the coffee industry.

WHY DRINK COFFEE?

☕Increased alertness and focus: Coffee contains caffeine, a stimulant that can help improve focus and concentration. This can be particularly helpful for students who need to stay alert during long study sessions or lectures.

☕Improved memory and cognitive function: Some studies have shown that caffeine can also help improve memory and cognitive function, which can be beneficial for students who need to retain a lot of information for exams or projects.

☕Boosted energy: Coffee can provide a quick energy boost that can help students power through their day, especially when they have a lot on their plate.

☕Social benefits: Coffee can also be a great way to connect with others, whether it's meeting up with friends at a local coffee shop or bonding with fellow coffee lovers in a student-led club like CoffeeHub.
